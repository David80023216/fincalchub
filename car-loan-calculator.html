How to Use the Car Loan Calculator

Our auto loan calculator helps you budget for your next vehicle purchase. Simply enter the vehicle price, your down payment, the interest rate offered by your lender, and the loan term. Click Calculate to instantly see your monthly payment and total loan cost.

Pro tip: Try different loan terms to see how they affect your monthly payment and total interest. A shorter term means higher payments but much less interest paid overall.

Auto Loan Payment Formula

Car loan payments use the same amortization formula as mortgages:

M = P × [r(1+r)ⁿ] ÷ [(1+r)ⁿ − 1]

Where: P = Loan amount (price − down payment), r = Monthly rate (APR ÷ 12), n = Number of monthly payments
